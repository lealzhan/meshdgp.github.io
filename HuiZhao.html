Research Statement

My research area is computer graphics. I particularly focus on Mesh Processing. I prefer designing computer graphics algorithms powered by math. Especially I use differiential geometry to solve the mesh processing problem.

I also work on rending, animation, etc.

I led a team and developed the MeshDGP: a Mesh Processing framework for students, developer and researchers with C#. It is well refacted, module based, including many key stone mesh processing algorithms.
